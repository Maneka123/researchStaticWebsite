Research Objectives

The research objectives aim to develop a comprehensive system for predicting cat diseases using machine learning algorithms, specifically decision tree classifiers and random forest models, to analyze symptoms. The system is designed to provide accurate and timely predictions of cat diseases, enhancing traditional diagnostic methods. Additionally, the research seeks to integrate a chatbot interface for easy access and use by veterinarians and pet owners, and incorporate Google Maps to help users locate nearby animal clinics and veterinarians.
